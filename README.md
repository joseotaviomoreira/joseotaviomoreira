## About Me
I am a 9th-grade student from Pernambuco, Brazil, building a solid foundation to become an **Automation Specialist** and researcher in **Quantum Computing**.

My interest in technology and programming began at age six, when I discovered it was possible to create my own games. Around age ten, I discovered the field of automation using Python and machine learning; I was captivated and fascinated by it—a feeling that only intensified when LLMs from OpenAI and Google became available to the general public.

My interest in quantum computing began recently, when I was introduced to the concepts and possibilities this technology unlocks. Since then, I have been studying modern physics and IBM's Qiskit.

Another area I enjoy is technology applied to energy management.
